Game Developer

Call of Duty: World at War was developed by Treyarch, a major video game studio known for its contributions to the Call of Duty franchise, including the Black Ops series. Treyarch is recognized for combining narrative-driven storytelling with fast-paced gameplay, helping shape the modern first-person shooter genre.

Key Voice Actors

Kiefer Sutherland

Kiefer Sutherland voices Sergeant Roebuck. He is widely known for his role in the television series 24 and has appeared in numerous films and voice acting roles. His performance adds emotional intensity and realism to the game’s narrative.

Gary Oldman

Gary Oldman voices Sergeant Reznov, one of the most memorable characters in the game. He is an Academy Award-winning actor known for roles in The Dark Knight, Harry Potter, and Darkest Hour. His performance contributes significantly to the game’s darker tone.

Craig Houston

Craig Houston contributed to the writing and narrative development of the game, helping shape its intense and historically inspired storyline. His work reflects the importance of storytelling in creating immersive gaming experiences.

Additional Contributions

The development of World at War involved collaboration among designers, programmers, artists, and writers. This highlights how modern video games are the result of complex technological and creative processes, combining multiple disciplines to produce immersive digital experiences.
