Game Developer

Call of Duty: World at War was developed by Treyarch, a major video game development studio known for its work on the Call of Duty series. Treyarch has contributed to multiple successful titles and is recognized for creating immersive and action-focused gameplay experiences.

Key Voice Actors

Kiefer Sutherland

Kiefer Sutherland voices Sergeant Roebuck in the game. He is a well-known actor famous for his role in the television series "24" and has appeared in numerous films and voice acting roles.

Gary Oldman

Gary Oldman voices Sergeant Reznov, one of the most memorable characters in the game. Oldman is an award-winning actor known for roles in films such as "The Dark Knight" and "Harry Potter."

Craig Houston

Craig Houston contributed to the writing and narrative development of the game, helping shape its darker and more intense portrayal of World War II.

Additional Contributions

The development of World at War involved a large team of designers, programmers, and artists who worked together to create a detailed and immersive experience. The collaboration between technical experts and creative professionals highlights how modern video games are the result of complex technological and artistic processes.
