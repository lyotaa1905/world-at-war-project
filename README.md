# Call of Duty: World at War – Technology and Society Project

This project analyzes the representation of World War II technology in *Call of Duty: World at War* and examines its accuracy and social impact.

## Topics Covered
- Military technology in WWII
- Accuracy of in-game weapons and combat
- Social and ethical implications of war technology
- Course concepts such as technological determinism and the military-industrial complex
